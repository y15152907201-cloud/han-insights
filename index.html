About Us

Selina Han

Founder & Chief Economist

Selina Han is the Founder of Han Insights, an independent research platform dedicated to helping professionals, investors, and organizations better understand the forces shaping financial markets and the global economy. With expertise in U.S. equity markets, listed options and derivatives, market structure, exchange economics, artificial intelligence, commodities, and macroeconomic trends, Selina combines rigorous research with real-world market experience to translate complex ideas into clear, actionable insights. Her work bridges finance, technology, and public policy, connecting developments across disciplines to explain not only what is happening in the markets, but why it matters. Before founding Han Insights, Selina served as an Economist at Cboe Global Markets, where she specialized in U.S. equity and derivatives markets, market structure, exchange economics, and financial market regulation. Han Insights was founded on the belief that the future belongs to those who can connect ideas across disciplines. By combining rigorous research with clear communication, Selina aims to make institutional-quality insights more accessible and inspire more thoughtful conversations about markets, technology, investing, and the future.
