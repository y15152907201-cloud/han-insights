Our Philosophy

Built on a simple principle.

Han Insights is built on a simple principle: Start with facts, not conclusions.

Rather than beginning with a predetermined narrative and searching for supporting evidence, we start with data, market observations, and industry developments. We then build a framework to explain what the evidence suggests.

Our goal is not to advocate for a company, product, exchange, asset manager, or investment position. Our goal is to understand how markets, technology, and capital allocation interact — and to help investors think more clearly about those dynamics.
